Data science is not dead.

But the title was always made up.

I audited the career using public numbers:

• 245,900 US data scientist jobs in 2024
• 34% projected growth by 2034
• ~10.97x faster than all occupations overall
• AI adoption jumped 55% → 78% in one year

The contradiction:

Data science is growing while its procedural workflow is being automated.

So the weak version dies:
clean data → model → dashboard → slide deck.

The strong version survives:
frame the problem, understand the domain, validate the output, know when the metric is fake.

The title was made up.
The value was not.
